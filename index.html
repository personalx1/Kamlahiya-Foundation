The Kamlahiya Foundation is a remarkable organization dedicated to empowering physically challenged individuals through the organization and promotion of para sports. With a strong belief in the potential and abilities of every person, the foundation strives to create inclusive opportunities for individuals with disabilities to engage in athletic activities and showcase their talents. At the core of the Kamlahiya Foundation's mission is the recognition that sports have the power to transform lives, instill confidence, and foster a sense of belonging. Through meticulous planning and tireless efforts, the foundation organizes a range of para sports events.



Join our cause

The aims and objectives of the NGO shall be as follows:-
•To organize the camps for disability awareness.
•To organize the camps for assistive aids distribution.
•To provide financial aid to the differently abled athletes(Para athletes) for their skill achievement.
•To create awareness about substance abuse and it’s adverse effects.
